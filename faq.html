FAQ

1. What programs or courses does your institute offer?
Our institute offers a wide range of programs including professional IT training, language courses, business management, and skill development programs tailored to students and professionals.
2. How can I apply for admission?
You can apply directly through our official website by filling out the online application form. Alternatively, you may visit our campus admission office for in-person assistance.
3. Do you provide online classes?
Yes, we provide both online and offline classes. Online classes are conducted through modern learning platforms with live sessions, recorded lectures, and interactive support.
4. What are the class timings?
Class timings are flexible. We offer morning, afternoon, and evening sessions to accommodate students, job holders, and professionals with different schedules.
5. Is there any scholarship or financial aid available?
Yes, we offer scholarships based on merit and financial need. Students with outstanding academic performance or limited financial resources can apply for financial aid during the admission process.
